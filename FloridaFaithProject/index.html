Why we started

Why we started

As of 2020, we are a group of sophomore students attending high school with one common goal, to bring help to the homeless. The COVID-19 pandemic has helped us realized how much we need to help others and stick together. Although we are in panic because of the pandemic, we must stay united and lend a helping hand to others. We might only be sophomores, but we hope to bring a big change among our community.

Why we started

Why we started

During the COVID-19 pandemic we realized the many adversities people came across, especially the homeless. We felt it was our duty to help those who need it and slow the rates of both infection and homelessness. By creating this project, we plan to collect money to help buy sanitation products and we hope to hold collection drives in order to provide the homeless with necessities such as food, clothing, hygiene products, and more.

Why we started

Our Mission

We hope that we can bring people together in order to create a better living for the homeless. We want to see improvement in the world and we want to come together to put a stop to homelessness. By organizing fundraisers, collections, and by spreading awareness we can slow down the rate of homelessness. But we can't do it alone! We need you to find ways to help your local homeless shelter as much as you can!
